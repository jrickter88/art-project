Erasmus of Rotterdam



By

Hans Holbein the Younger

He also painted the occasional portrait, making his international mark with portraits of the humanist Desiderius Erasmus of Rotterdam.
When the Reformation reached Basel, Holbein worked for reformist clients while continuing to serve traditional religious patrons.



Date: 1523
Medium: Oil and tempera on wood
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: National Gallery, London
